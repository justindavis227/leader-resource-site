Leader
Commitment

Serving with minors is a trusted leadership role. The following commitments define who we commit to be and what we commit to do so students are discipled, cared for, and protected with integrity.

What We Commit to Be
01
Christ Follower
I acknowledge Jesus Christ as Lord and will keep growing through the Bible, prayer, worship, and community in the life of the church.
02
Representative
I represent Jesus, Southeast Christian Church, Southeast Students and will support the church’s mission, beliefs, leadership, and unity.
03
Above Reproach
I will live with integrity in my words, actions, relationships, habits, and online presence, and will not knowingly misrepresent Jesus or Southeast.
04
Trustworthy
I will use my influence to protect, encourage, and disciple students—never to manipulate, isolate, shame, favor, or create inappropriate dependence.
05
Teachable
I will receive coaching, correction, and direction with humility. Teachability is part of faithful leadership.
06
Disciple-Maker
This is more than a weekly program. I will pray for students, help them feel known, and point them to Jesus and their next steps of faith.
07
Integrity
If my ability to lead with integrity becomes compromised, I will speak with Staff and, if needed, step back from serving.
08
Conflict Resolution
Following Matthew 18:15-17, I will handle conflict with humility—going directly to the person, avoiding gossip, pursuing reconciliation, and involving the right leaders when needed.

What We Commit to Do

01
Serve Faithfully
I will serve the full ministry year—August through July—and participate in weekly gatherings, leader trainings, and all required ministry rhythms.
02
Consistent
I will be on time, prepared, and engaged when serving. Consistency builds trust with students, families, leaders, and staff.
03
Communicate
If I need to miss a gathering, event, or serving commitment, I will tell my co-leaders and Staff as early as possible.
04
Be a Teammate
I will work with co-leaders, campus staff, parents, and SE Students leadership in a way that is clear, cooperative, and aligned.
05
Safety Guidelines
I will follow all Southeast and Southeast Students safety policies and boundaries at all times, including:
Never being alone with a student in a private or isolated setting.
Appropriate physical, emotional, spiritual, relational, and digital boundaries.
Communicating with students only in transparent and approved ways.
Reporting concerns, disclosures, or unsafe situations to Staff immediately.
Agreement

By signing, I confirm I have read, understand, and agree to these commitments as a leader with Southeast Students. Failure to follow these commitments may result in being paused or removed from serving. If I have questions or cannot agree to any commitment, I will speak with Staff before serving.
