Southeast Students · Luke 9 Ministry Team

Spring Semester Planning

The culture of our ministry to students starts with you, our leaders, living like Jesus.
We simply want to do what He did and allow His life to inform our ministry decisions and direction.

Luke 9  ·  The intentional rhythms of Jesus’ ministry
Rhythm 1Jesus called the Twelve together.Community
Rhythm 2Jesus equipped them with power and authority.Equipping
Rhythm 3Jesus sent them out to go from town to town.Mission
Rhythm 4Jesus invited his disciples to care for the crowd.Care
Rhythm 5Jesus retreated with his disciples to pray.Retreat

With Luke 9 in mind, let’s make some intentional plans for our ministry to students this semester:

Calendar
Ministry Dates →Review our calendar.
Team Hangouts →Set up some time(s) to be together.
Team Meetings →Plan our monthly team meetings.
Small Groups →Anything outside of regular ministry time.
Goal Planning
New Students
How many new students do we want to see in our ministry?
What is our follow up plan for new students?
Camp
How many unconnected students are we praying for at camp?
Who are our student leaders and how could we set them up to lead with us?
Students Serving
How many students do we have currently serving in some capacity?
How many of our students are ready to serve, but aren’t currently?
Baptism
How many students baptizing their friends are we praying for?
How could we keep track of our students’ “ones” and be praying for them?
