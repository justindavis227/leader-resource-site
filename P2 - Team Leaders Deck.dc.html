Team Leader
Expectations 1–3
1Live the MissionYou set the pace for how your team uniquely lives the mission to your students. If you are passive, your leaders will be passive—making students passive.
2Meet with LeadersPray for, care for and coach leaders on your team. Ask about their walk with Christ, their life, and their ministry using the Key Conversations. Pray together // call to action.
3Own Student ConnectionsEnsure students are invited regularly. With Guest Experience, have a plan for connecting new students to the right group and leader. Know the groups within your team.

Team Leader
Expectations 4–7
4Lead Team MeetingsLead spiritually—bring God’s Word, the life of Christ, 4 Chairs, our mission and values into conversations often. Organize calendar dates. Lead Semester Planning (2x / year). Pray, Plan, Prepare, Pursue.
5Live the MantrasEnsure you and the team are embodying our mantras. Set measurable goals and hold the team accountable.
6Encourage Investment in a FewLead by example on investing in a few. Hold the team accountable to identifying and discipling a few students.
7Know Your Camp StrategyLead your team’s strategy for “who’s your one?” Prepare your team for next steps and follow up post camp.
