Southeast Students · 4-Chair Personal Inventory

Where Am I?

The “four chairs” picture the progressive stages of a disciple. Answer honestly—score each statement, then total each chair in the scoring column.

Chair 1 · Seeker—“Come & See”Disagree 1 · Unsure 2 · Agree 3
  • 1I am confident I will be with God in heaven after I die.
  • 2I could never be good enough to earn my way to heaven.
  • 3Religious acts don’t make me a Christian.
  • 4There is only one way to God—faith in His Son, Jesus.
  • 5I’ve put my faith in Christ’s death and resurrection as the only payment for my sin.
  • 6My commitment to follow Christ is a response to grace.
  • 7Those who don’t put their faith in Christ will be separated from God for eternity.
Chair 2 · Believer—“Follow Me”Weak 1 · Improving 2 · Strong 3
  • 8I am developing the character of Christ (Gal 5:22–23).
  • 9I am developing the priorities of Christ (Matt 22:37–39).
  • 10I put Christ first in every area of my life.
  • 11I depend on God’s Spirit for strength and direction.
  • 12I spend time with God daily in His Word and prayer.
  • 13I know my spiritual gifts and use them to serve.
  • 14I’m concerned for those who don’t know Jesus.
Chair 3 · Worker—“Fish for People”Weak 1 · Improving 2 · Strong 3
  • 15I pursue relationships with lost people and pray for their salvation.
  • 16I know how to communicate my salvation story.
  • 17I can share the gospel simply and clearly (in two minutes).
  • 18I act on opportunities to share the gospel verbally.
  • 19I pursue discipling relationships and pray for others’ growth.
  • 20I know the basics a new believer needs to grow.
  • 21I share what God is teaching me to encourage others.
Chair 4 · Disciple-Maker—“Bear Much Fruit”Weak 1 · Improving 2 · Strong 3
  • 22I’ve shown others how to share their faith & they’re doing it.
  • 23I regularly meet with a believer to show them how to follow Christ.
  • 24I walk with others toward becoming disciples who make disciples.
  • 25I model a disciple-making lifestyle that inspires others.
  • 26I prioritize time with lost people and disciple believers.
  • 27I’ve discipled someone who is now discipling another.
  • 28I initiate new relationships to make disciples.
Scoring

Total each chair below. A score of 20–21 means you can focus on the next chair while staying strong in the ones before.
